About Me

I am currently a Ph.D. student at the Multimedia Laboratory (MMLab), The Chinese Univerty of Hong Kong. My research advisor is Prof. Hongsheng Li.

I received the B. E. degree from the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, Shanghai Jiao Tong University in 2023, ranking 2/119 in the major, and awarded with the National Scholarship in 2020, 2021 and 2022.

My research interests include model compression, multimodal large language models, and AI4Science.
